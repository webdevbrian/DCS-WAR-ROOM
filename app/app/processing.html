Pilot Identifier

EXAMPLE: Adding "Phrozen" and "Brian" will match a SINGLE pilot to any callsign containing any capitializations of either, but either containing specifically "Phrozen" and "Brian".

NOTE: It is not required to provide both, just the first is required at minimum. You can add two if someone changed their actual nickname (after their callsign for example) or do other clever matches using these two provided fields.
