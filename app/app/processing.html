Here you associate your pilots by the handles they use. You want to avoid using a full and specific name association.

Pilot Identifier

EXAMPLE: Adding "Phrozen" and "Brian" will match a SINGLE pilot to any callsign containing any capitializations of either, but either containing specifically "Phrozen" and "Brian".

NOTE: It is not required to provide both, just the first is required at minimum. You can add two if someone changed their actual nickname (before callsigns) or do other clever matches using these two provided fields.
